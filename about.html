About DI

Diversified Industries C & IS Inc. is a Minority Owned business offering customers’ a competitive edge by leveraging our engineering experience in conjunction with Lean processes with a robust software tool. This combination delivers a significant time savings and improves and provides consistency to the quality, safety, and reliability of work being done.

We represent a broad cross section of talent across the United States with many decades of combined industry experience in design, test, checkout and maintenance of flight hardware, Ground Support Equipment (GSE) systems and large public transportation buses.

We are pleased and fortunate to be able to share our strength of accomplishments and our industry experience over many combined years. Most important to us is the shared integrity by DI team members throughout business and our communities.

Following the formal announcement of the Space Shuttle Program cancellation by President G. W. Bush in 2006, Martin Belson knew as a Shuttle employee at the Kennedy Space Center (KSC) in Florida that there would be much great talent loss from the American Space Program. He began to take notice of the rich NASA Inspired technology and talent that surrounded him. Martin began to lay out a plan. The first important asset realized was the outstanding quality of the people that he worked with for many years- their work ethics, commitment, dependability, skill and their unique experiences were some of the best in the world. There was no doubt that this was a winning combination that would serve him to create employment for himself and other KSC workers. Second, as one of the co-inventors of the SMART software, Martin applied for, and was awarded by NASA, exclusive technology rights to market and sell the SMART software following his retirement from KSC in October of 2010. That is where the NASA Inspired SMART software commercializing began.

About Our Team

  • Martin A. Belson - Founder, President and CEO.

    Martin A. Belson is the President, founder, and CEO of Diversified Industries C & IS incorporated, as well as a co-inventor of the SMART Solution software. He began his professional career as a mechanical design engineer. He brings over 30 years of experience in integrated system test engineering, project engineering and project management. As a successful company technology manager, he has helped liberate many employees' creative talents as well as capture and package new processes, technology, and inventions for which they were recognized and rewarded.

  • John O. Hampton - Consultant

    John is the former Shuttle Systems Chief Engineer at United Space Alliance and has over 30 years of experience in electrical communications, working for companies such as USBI, Morton Thiokol, Lockheed Martin, and United Space Alliance, as well as the U.S. Air Force.

  • Bill Peters - COO

    Bill has a degree in Aerospace Engineering and holds an Airframe and Powerplant technician certification. He retired from United Space Alliance as an Orbiter Electrical Engineer where he used SMART to assist with wiring repairs on America's Space Shuttle fleet.

With over 30 years of Aviation & Defense experience his operations and business management leadership provides many benefits to our customers.
